FinTec Consulting

consulting. done right

Who we are?

The team at FinTec Consulting consists of people with years of experience in Financial Solution consulting and transformation. We specialize in Lending, Treasury, Trade Finance and Payments domain. Our people bring unrivalled expertise and technical skills, including Project Management, Requirement Analysis and Testing. Our people are creative, meticulous and come from a wide variety of backgrounds in the Financial Industry.

What we do?

We enable financial institutions to take that big leap in technology change and process improvement. We become a part of your organization, understand your business and help you choose the best solution for your technological leap. We help you reach there. Together!

How we do it

Services

Project Management

We believe Project Management is an art of ensuring things are done right the first time. We have experience implementing projects using Agile, Waterfall and a combination of both. Or consultants have years of experience doing both requirement gathering and project management simultaneously. We believe, a project manager must know the business to be able to make the right call. We understand what we do.

Requirement Gathering

We help solve the biggest challenge in any project, figure out what you want. We are story tellers who will help you define your requirements to the point and ensure nothing is lost in transit. We will help you document your requirements, draw down a plan of implementation and help you execute it. We not only help you gather requirements but also guide you on the best practices and how to efficiently carry out business.

Testing and Support

Our Consultants are seasoned functional testers. They not only gather requirements on your behalf but also help your end users prepare test cases to ensure all aspects of requirements are tested. This is done by validating the test cases prepared by the end users against the requirement document and the outcome of the UAT. Post GO-Live support is provided for seamless transition.
