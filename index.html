FinTec Consulting

consulting. done right

Who we are?

FinTec Consulting is a management and technology consulting firm focused on helping you improve, transform and build your company. Enabling financial institutions to take that big leap in technology change and process improvement is our forté. With a distinctive ability to rapidly collaborate on your strategy and play a leadership role in helping you to execute it, we are not only Consultants, we view ourselves as part of your company. Your goals are our goals.

What we do?

It’s all about our Clients!
We understand that trust is the currency of the financial market. Cooperation, not Disruption is what digitization of the finance industry is all about. You will receive specialized care for strategic execution from experts that have been a part of the biggest names in Fin-tech. We specialize in Lending, Treasury, Trade Finance and Payments domain.

How we do it

Services

Project Management

We help you create products with strong user adoption and an exponential revenue growth. Our expertise lies in identifying a market problem, quantifying the opportunity to make sure it’s big enough to generate profit, and then articulate the problem to the rest of your company.
We communicate market opportunities to the executive team including financial forecasts and risk assessment. We communicate the problem to Development team in the form of market requirements; to Marketing team using positioning documents, one for each type of buyer; we support the Sales effort by defining a sales process supported by the requisite sales tools such that your customer can choose the right products and options.
A product is never finished. There is no right product, but we know there sure is the right means to Product Management.

Product Management

We believe Product Management is an art of ensuring things are done right the first time. We have experience implementing projects using Agile, Waterfall and a combination of both.
For us, good project management means much more than keeping the iron triangle of project management in check—delivering on time, budget, and scope; we unite teams and clients, create a vision for success and bring everyone on the same page of what is needed to stay on track for success. Our experts understand, projects when managed properly have a positive impact that reverberates beyond delivery of ‘the stuff’.

Requirement Gathering

We help solve the biggest challenge in any project, figuring out what you want.
By enabling a clear and precise definition of the scope of a project, we break down the business needs into specific tasks to help assess the resources needed to complete a project.
We are storytellers who will help you break down a project into small elements to focus on one element at a time. This helps to easily identify the lack of information, and understand where further analysis is required. Documenting your requirements, drawing down a plan of implementation and execution of the plan was never simpler!
Our experts will also guide you with the best practices and demonstrate how to efficiently carry out your business.

Testing and Support

We have seasoned functional testers who understand that ink is better than memory. That’s right!
Right from gathering requirements, they also help your users write test cases to ensure all aspects of requirements are tested. This is done by validating the test cases prepared by the end users against the requirement document and the outcome of the User Acceptance Testing (UAT).
Going-live need not be a high stress transition as many believe it to be. With FinTec Consulting you will have ‘at-the-elbow’ go-live support from trained support professionals who have a wealth of experience seeing companies through this critical time and can help you avoid common pitfalls. We are there to support you and make go-live remarkably smoother.

Technical Documentation

When documentation is an afterthought, business award decisions can become big, costly regrets. Our team has Technical Documentation experts who understand that the secret sauce for communication is not just the transfer of words, it is in the transfer of meaning.
Our writers have spent years crafting the art of communicating a body of factual information that will help your audience understand a subject or carry out a task, irrespective of the complexity, for a global audience. Well-written technical documentation convey that you care enough about the details to get it right. And that says everything about you as a company! With our help, your documentation is not just a “help”—it becomes a valuable Business Asset.
